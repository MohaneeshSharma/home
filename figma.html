Seat Types & Team Allocation

Different team members need different levels of access. Here's how we can allocate seats:

Collab Seat

  • Ideal for stakeholders, product managers, and marketing team members
  • Brainstorm in FigJam
  • Present in Figma Slides
  • Comment and review designs
  • View all team projects

Dev Seat

  • Perfect for developers and engineers
  • All Collab seat capabilities
  • Build directly in Dev Mode
  • Promote in Figma Buzz
  • Detailed code inspection and export
  • Handoff designs to development

Full Seat

  • Essential for designers and design leads
  • All Dev seat capabilities
  • Create in Figma Design
  • Illustrate in Figma Draw
  • Publish in Figma Sites
  • Explore in Figma Make
  • Complete design system management
