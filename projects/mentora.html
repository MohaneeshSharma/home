UX Case Study

Mentora.

Bridging the gap between campus and career by building trust and verified connections for Subharti University.

Role Product Designer
Platform iOS & Android
Timeline 8 Weeks
Tools Figma, Miro

Mentora

The Challenge

Students feel lost after graduation. Existing platforms are too broad, lacking the specific trust factor needed for mentorship.

Problem Statement

Students at Subharti University often face a "guidance void" as they approach graduation. While they have academic knowledge, they lack industry insights. Existing platforms like LinkedIn feel too vast and impersonal, leading to "cold messaging" anxiety.

The Core Need

  • Students need a safe, verified space to ask questions without fear of judgment.
  • Alumni want to give back but lack a time-efficient way to do so.

Landscape

LinkedIn

Good for jobs, but intimidating for students.

WhatsApp

High engagement, but chaotic & unverified.

Mentora

Verified, university-specific, & structured.

SWOT Analysis

Strengths

Exclusive verification (Roll No) builds instant trust.

Weaknesses

"Cold start" problem; needs users to populate content.

Opportunities

Partnership with university placement cell.

Threats

User inertia; convincing alumni to use another app.

User Research

We surveyed 65 students to understand the emotional barriers to networking.
