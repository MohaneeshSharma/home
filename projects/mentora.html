Mentora is a community-driven app designed to connect students and alumni of Subharti University. The vision is to create a digital mentorship ecosystem where students can learn from alumni experiences, find career guidance, discover job opportunities, and build meaningful professional relationships. This was my capstone project during the upGrad + KnowledgeHut UI/UX Bootcamp, where I applied the full user-centered design process â€” from research to prototyping.

ðŸ”Ž Problem Statement

Subharti University students often face challenges such as a lack of structured alumni connections, limited access to career mentorship, and difficulty staying updated about networking opportunities. There was no central platform to bridge students with the vast alumni network.

ðŸ’¡ My Process

1. Research

I conducted Google Form surveys with students & alumni and discovered key pain points: students want career advice & internship opportunities, while alumni want to give back to the university but lack a proper channel.

2. Defining the MVP

Based on the research, the core features were defined:

  • Verified Connections: Students can connect only with real alumni.
  • Career Section: Jobs, internships, and career blogs curated by alumni.
  • Events: University & alumni-hosted sessions.
  • Blogs & Stories: Alumni share experiences for student motivation.
  • Connect Hub: Direct mentorship via chat/request.

3. Information Architecture & User Flows

I designed persona-driven user journeys for both students and alumni and created task flows for key actions like connection requests, event registrations, and job applications.

4. Design Execution

I built wireframes to validate the structure and then designed a high-fidelity prototype in Figma with a modern, minimal UI, focusing on mobile-first accessibility for students.

ðŸ“ˆ Outcome

The result was a working Figma prototype showcasing the entire student-alumni experience, which received positive feedback from mentors and peers during the bootcamp. The project validated that Mentora has the potential to scale into a real community platform for Subharti University.
