About Me

Hi, I’m Mohineesh Sharma, a passionate UI/UX designer and technology consultant with a focus on creating intuitive, human-centered digital experiences. I thrive at the intersection of design, strategy, and mentorship, helping products not just look good but feel seamless and impactful.

With experience in EdTech, B2B platforms, and corporate design projects, I specialize in user research, prototyping, design systems, and UX storytelling. Beyond designing, I mentor aspiring designers, guiding them to solve real-world problems and build portfolios that stand out.

I believe every design should solve a problem, delight users, and drive results. When I’m not crafting experiences, I explore AI-driven design tools, emerging trends, and creative storytelling.

Let’s create something meaningful together.
