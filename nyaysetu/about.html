Guiding Principles

Integrity First

We verify every single profile manually. If a lawyer isn't registered with the Bar Council, they aren't on NyayaSetu.

Absolute Privacy

Legal matters are sensitive. We use end-to-end encryption. Your data is never sold, shared, or compromised.

Neutrality

We don't "rank" lawyers based on payments. Discovery is based purely on expertise, location, and verified experience.
